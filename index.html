30th June 2017 - Major Patch v260, Tek Trough, more server options and more fixes!

22th June 2017 - Major Patch v259, Forced Survivor Restat and loads of fixes!

So Major Patch version 259 has just dropped. We have updated Neanderthal Extreme PvE server and a wild dino wipe has taken place. Upon logging in you may have noticed that your survivor level has been reset back to level 1 and you have been refunded all of you points. This is due to "Crafting Speed" being replaced with "Crafting Skill". Very little is know about this atm but essentially by adding point to the new "Crafting Skill" you stand a chance of crafting a better item from your Blueprints. Also fishing has been "rebalanced" so you may notice that any fishing rods you had may be gone.

Rules...

Main overall rule is to NOT be a dick!

  • Be friendly
  • Don't interfere with anyones tame
  • Don't pick anyone up without permission
  • Destroy any unused Campfire's/Raft's/Structure's (this will help with lag)
  • Don't build on or block access to any of the Obelisk's
  • Don't build close to others (unless you have permission)
  • Try not to block off coastal pathways
  • Try not to build in resource rich area's
  • Don't block any of the cave entrance's
  • Please empty Beaver Dams (Otherwise they won't respawn)
  • Do have a good time and stay safe
